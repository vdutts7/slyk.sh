## ğŸš€Usage

1. **Configure** your keyboard shortcut in Settings
2. **Press** the shortcut to start recording
3. **Speak** your words
4. **Release** and text appears in your active app

### Modes

- **Toggle mode** - Press once to start, press again to stop
- **Push-to-talk** - Hold to record, release to transcribe

### Settings

- Custom keyboard shortcuts
- Model selection (speed vs accuracy)
- Language selection
- Audio feedback sounds
- Clipboard handling options
- History management

## ğŸ§ Models

### Whisper Models (GPU accelerated)

| Model | Size | Speed | Accuracy |
|-------|------|-------|----------|
| Small | ~500MB | Fast | Good |
| Medium | ~1.5GB | Medium | Better |
| Turbo | ~800MB | Fast | Great |
| Large | ~3GB | Slow | Best |

### Parakeet V3 (CPU optimized)

- **No GPU required** - runs on CPU
- **~5x realtime** on mid-range hardware
- **Auto language detection** - no manual selection needed
- Best for older machines or when GPU is busy
